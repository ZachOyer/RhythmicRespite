Intro

I wrote this poem for people like you
Intrigued to hear the rhymes that I do
Never sure which one to read you first
So I fused my thoughts into this verse
I've hundreds of interests, but excel at not one
A jack of all trades, and a master of none
A dabble in this and I dally in that
I avoid hard tasks like an acrobat
I hate most things that require my work
But my hunger for perfection is quite a jerk
My cat makes me feel of glee and glad
My murder mitten'd Midna in mightnight clad
If you saw what I eat you'd think I was fat
But I stave of size with a wall and a mat
It used to be, that fast I could run
But I ditched that shit for something more fun
Instead of sloshing around my insides
I climb up heights which thrills and excites
And now you know the things I've been through
Enough about me, lets hear about you
