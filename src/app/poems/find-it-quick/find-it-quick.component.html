Find It Quick

Fun fact
This poem is in the form of a Villanelle, with a few minor changes.
Searching far and wide for my hidden might
I must find it quick while I've got time
Cherish the day, while fearing the night

The strength I want won't help in a fight
Because its the strength to be alone
Searching deep within for my hidden might

The sun reveals friends with its light
The moon closes doors and masks my eyes
Cherish the day, while fearing the night

Scouring under the waves, beyond left and right
I hold my breath through my perilous dive
Searching deep below for my hidden might

The dawn uncovers paths with futures bright
But the dread of dusk obscures my options
Cherish the day, while fearing the night

I'm putting in work to resolve my plight
I've won when I know its never too late
Searching deep inside for my hidden might
Cherish the day, embracing the night
