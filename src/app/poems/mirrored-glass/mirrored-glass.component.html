Mirrored Glass
The mirrored glass is the view we need to live our lives with. The mirror allows us to see ourselves, never forgetting to think about us and our role in the world. The the transluscency of the glass allows us to see others as well. Our thoughts should consider both ourselves and those around us.

And at first glance of the glass, I see my mirror
Only to mistake it for the universal gateway at my disposal
The rainbow reflection that parades any colors one could possible desire
An enticing entrance to the factual or fictional life of another
Viewed through the same lens that I watch my own
Muddling the lines between theirs and mine
Misguiding me to entertain the idea
That I don't need ours when I have theirs

Yet I have the chance to pick my place
Stepping back and forth in an existential dance
Aligning my reflection with where I want it to be
Amidst the picture of people and places
Piecing together the pearlescent puzzle in the glass
And when I find the perfect distance
I become my reflection, and my reflection becomes me
Surrounded by sympathetic souls with open hearts
In a place of abundant belonging - where I'm meant to be
Free to traverse beyond the edges of before
While my reflection - returned to his ephemeral cage
Doomed to vanish at a couple of my steps...
But he's not overwhelmed with vehement rage
Instead, I see him smile
I see Me smile
