Missed Opportunity

"I'm gonna be late!" Was his fleeting thought
With embarrassing entrances was seminar fraught
But he entered and met with beautiful eyes
A beckoning hand and welcoming guise
She invited him in to sit and wait
Her sideward seat fertile with fate
"No thanks" he said and denied her hand
"I'm here to sit with my previous band"
He slipped her by like wind through her hair
His mind as empty as the chair
But that's not all that happened to pass
He wasn't done proving himself an ass
With seminar over, and debates all around
She approached once more with her courage found
"I like your shirt," yet her words fall flat
Not reaching the clouds where his head is at
She left her suitor with naught but a "Thanks!"
Definitively deposited in his memory banks
And while this moment doesn't make him cry
Remembering definitely makes him want to die
